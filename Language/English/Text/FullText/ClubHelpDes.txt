New a match
1. New match in club does not consume the room card, Players can invite friends to participate in the event for free.
2. Players can enter the registration event through the invitation link or the competition password, and can only participate in the competition with the consent of the host.
3. The scoreboard is used to record the situation of the game. It will not be taken out of the room or exchanged for gold coins after the game is over.
4. When the number of participants is less than 2 at the start of the match, the match will be automatically disbanded.
